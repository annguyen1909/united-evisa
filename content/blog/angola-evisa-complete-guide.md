# Angola eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Angola?

Angola is a country of stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and diverse landscapes. From the modern capital Luanda to the majestic Kalandula Falls and the historic M'banza-Kongo UNESCO site, Angola offers unforgettable travel experiences. Whether you are interested in adventure, history, or vibrant city life, Angola is a must-visit destination in Africa.

## Angola eVisa Overview

The Angola eVisa is an electronic travel authorization that allows eligible foreign nationals to enter Angola for tourism or business. The eVisa system streamlines the application process, making it possible to apply entirely online without visiting an embassy or consulate.

## Who Needs an Angola eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Angola. The eVisa system is available to travelers from eligible countries. Some nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Angola eVisa Requirements

To apply for an Angola eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Angola
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English or Portuguese. Additional documents may be required for business travelers.
