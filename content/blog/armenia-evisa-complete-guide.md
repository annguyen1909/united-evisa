## Armenia eVisa Overview

The Armenia eVisa is an electronic travel authorization that allows eligible foreign nationals to enter Armenia for tourism or business. The eVisa system streamlines the application process, making it possible to apply entirely online without visiting an embassy or consulate.

## Who Needs an Armenia eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Armenia. The eVisa system is available to travelers from eligible countries. Some nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Armenia eVisa Requirements

To apply for an Armenia eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Armenia
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required for business travelers.
