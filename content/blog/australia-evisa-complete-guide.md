# Australia eVisa: Complete Guide (2025)

## Why visit Australia
Australia is a land of breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cities, and unique wildlife. Whether you're planning a holiday, business trip, or family visit, the Australia eVisa makes your journey Down Under easier than ever. This guide covers everything you need to know about the Australia eVisa, including requirements, visa types, application steps, fees, and travel tips.

## Overview
Australia welcomes millions of visitors each year with its world-famous attractions like the Sydney Opera House, Great Barrier Reef, and the Outback. The eVisa system streamlines the process for eligible travelers, allowing you to apply online and receive your visa electronically—no embassy visits required.

**Capital:** Canberra  
**Official Language:** English  
**Currency:** Australian Dollar (AUD)  
**Region:** Asia Pacific

## Who Needs an Australia eVisa?
Most foreign nationals require an eVisa to enter Australia, except for citizens of certain visa-waiver countries. The eVisa is available for tourism and business purposes, with multiple entries allowed within the visa validity period.

## Australia eVisa Types
- **Tourist eVisa (Multiple Entries for 1 Year):**
  - Valid for 1 year, allows multiple entries
  - Stay up to 60 days per visit
  - For tourism, sightseeing, family visits, and holidays
- **Business eVisa (Multiple Entries for 1 Year):**
  - Valid for 1 year, allows multiple entries
  - Stay up to 60 days per visit
  - For business meetings, conferences, and trade activities

## Required Documents
- Valid passport (at least 6 months validity)
- Recent passport-sized photo
- Travel itinerary
- Proof of accommodation
- Proof of sufficient funds
- Return or onward flight ticket
- **For business eVisa:**
  - Business invitation letter
  - Company registration or supporting documents

## Australia eVisa Application Process
1. **Complete the Online Application:** Fill out the eVisa form with your personal and travel details.
2. **Upload Documents:** Attach all required documents, including passport and photo.
3. **Pay Fees:** Pay the government and service fees securely online.
4. **Receive eVisa:** Get your approved eVisa via email (usually within 7 working days).

## Fees & Processing Times
- **Government Fee:** $150 USD (for both Tourist and Business eVisa)
- **Processing Time:**
  - Normal: 7 working days
  - Super Urgent: 2 working days (additional fee may apply)

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
**How long can I stay in Australia with an eVisa?**  
Up to 60 days per entry, with multiple entries allowed during the visa validity period.

**Can I work in Australia with a tourist eVisa?**  
No, the tourist eVisa does not permit employment. For work, apply for the appropriate visa category.

**What if my eVisa application is rejected?**  
You can reapply after addressing the issues mentioned in the rejection notice, or contact our support team for assistance.

**How far in advance should I apply?**  
Apply at least 2 weeks before your travel date to allow for processing.

**Can I extend my stay?**  
No, eVisa stays cannot be extended. You must leave Australia before your 60-day period expires.
