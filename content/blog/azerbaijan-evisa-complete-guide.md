# Azerbaijan eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Azerbaijan?

Azerbaijan is a country of rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes. From the modern cityscape of Baku to the ancient walled city, mud volcanoes, and the Caspian Sea coastline, Azerbaijan offers a unique blend of East and West. Whether you're interested in architecture, cuisine, or natural wonders, Azerbaijan is a must-visit destination in the Caucasus region.

## Azerbaijan eVisa Overview

The Azerbaijan eVisa system, introduced in 2017, allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This streamlined process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Azerbaijan more convenient and efficient.

## Who Needs an Azerbaijan eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Azerbaijan. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, or medical treatment. Some nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Azerbaijan eVisa Requirements

To apply for an Azerbaijan eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Azerbaijan
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents
- **For treatment eVisa:** Medical appointment or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your visa type.

## Travel Tips for Azerbaijan

- **Best Time to Visit:** Spring (March–May) and autumn (September–November) offer pleasant weather. Summer is great for mountain regions; winter is ideal for skiing.
- **Popular Destinations:** Baku, Gobustan, Sheki, Ganja, Caspian Sea coast, mud volcanoes.
- **Local Language:** Azerbaijani
- **Currency:** Azerbaijani Manat (AZN)
- **Safety:** Azerbaijan is generally safe for tourists. Exercise standard precautions and stay informed about travel advisories.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the State Migration Service for more information.
