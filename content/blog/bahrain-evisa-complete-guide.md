# Bahrain eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Bahrain?

Bahrain is a vibrant island nation in the Middle East, known for its rich history, modern skyline, and beautiful beaches. From ancient forts and bustling souqs to luxury shopping and Formula 1 racing, Bahrain offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity for every traveler.

## Bahrain eVisa Overview

The Bahrain eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Bahrain easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Bahrain eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Bahrain. The eVisa is available to travelers from over 100 countries for tourism or business. Some nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

- **Multiple Entries (3 months):** For frequent business trips. Government fee: $73 USD.
- **Multiple Entries (1 year):** For long-term business and frequent trips. Government fee: $147 USD.

## Bahrain eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Bahrain eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Bahrain
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English or Arabic. Additional documents may be required depending on your visa type.

## Travel Tips for Bahrain

- **Best Time to Visit:** November to March offers pleasant weather for sightseeing and outdoor activities.
- **Popular Destinations:** Manama, Bahrain Fort (Qal'at al-Bahrain), Bahrain National Museum, Amwaj Islands, Al Fateh Grand Mosque.
- **Local Language:** Arabic (English widely spoken)
- **Currency:** Bahraini Dinar (BHD)
- **Safety:** Bahrain is generally safe for tourists. Exercise standard precautions and respect local customs.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the NPRA office for more information.
