# Cambodia eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Cambodia?

Cambodia is a land of ancient temples, lush landscapes, and vibrant cities. From the iconic Angkor Wat to the lively streets of Phnom Penh, Cambodia offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler. Whether you're interested in history, culture, or adventure, Cambodia is a must-visit destination in Southeast Asia.

## Cambodia eVisa Overview

The Cambodia eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Cambodia easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Cambodia eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Cambodia. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, or transit. Some ASEAN citizens may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Cambodia eVisas

### Tourist eVisa

## Cambodia eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Cambodia eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Cambodia
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

## Travel Tips for Cambodia

- **Best Time to Visit:** The dry season (November–April) is ideal for temple exploration and sightseeing. The wet season (May–October) brings lush landscapes but more rain.
- **Popular Destinations:** Angkor Wat, Siem Reap, Phnom Penh, Sihanoukville, Tonle Sap Lake.
- **Local Language:** Khmer (English widely spoken in tourist areas)
- **Currency:** Cambodian Riel (KHR)
- **Safety:** Cambodia is generally safe for tourists. Exercise standard precautions and respect local customs.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the Immigration Department in Phnom Penh for more information.
