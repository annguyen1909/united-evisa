## Canada eVisa Overview

The Canada eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Canada easier and more efficient for eligible travelers.

## Who Needs a Canada eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Canada. However, citizens of visa-exempt countries like the United States, United Kingdom, and some European countries do not need a visa. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, or transit purposes.

## Canada eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Canada eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Canada
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Proof of sufficient funds (minimum $1,000 CAD per month)
- Travel insurance (recommended)
- Proof of ties to your home country

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English or French. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.
