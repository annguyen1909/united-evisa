# Egypt eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Egypt?

Egypt is a land of ancient wonders, from the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx to the Nile River and vibrant cities like Cairo and Luxor. Whether you're interested in history, culture, or adventure, Egypt offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

## Egypt eVisa Overview

The Egypt eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Egypt easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs an Egypt eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Egypt. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism purposes. Some Arab countries may be exempt from visa requirements, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Egypt eVisas

### Tourist eVisa (Single Entry)
- **Single Entry (30 days):** For tourism, cultural visits, and sightseeing. Government fee: $75 USD.

### Tourist eVisa (Multiple Entries)
- **Multiple Entries (30 days):** For extended stays, business trips, and family visits. Government fee: $110 USD.

## Egypt eVisa Requirements

To apply for an Egypt eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Egypt
- Return flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

## Entry Points for Egypt eVisa

Egypt eVisa holders can enter through Cairo International Airport, Hurghada, Sharm El Sheikh, and other designated ports of entry. Always carry your eVisa approval letter and passport when traveling.

## Travel Tips for Egypt

- **Best Time to Visit:** October to April offers cooler weather for sightseeing. Summer (May-September) can be very hot, especially in southern Egypt.
- **Popular Destinations:** Cairo, Luxor, Aswan, Alexandria, Red Sea resorts, and the Nile River.
- **Local Language:** Modern Standard Arabic (English widely spoken in tourist areas)
- **Currency:** Egyptian Pound (EGP)
- **Safety:** Egypt is generally safe for tourists, especially in popular areas. Stay informed about travel advisories and take standard precautions.
- **Cultural Sites:** Visit the Pyramids, Sphinx, Valley of the Kings, and ancient temples.
- **Nile Cruises:** A unique way to explore Egypt's history and landscapes.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the Immigration Department in Cairo or other major cities.
