# Ethiopia eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Ethiopia?

Ethiopia is a land of ancient history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant culture. From the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the Simien Mountains and bustling Addis Ababa, Ethiopia offers unique experiences for every traveler.

## Ethiopia eVisa Overview

The Ethiopia eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Ethiopia easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs an Ethiopia eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Ethiopia. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism purposes. Some African countries may be exempt from visa requirements, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Ethiopia eVisas

### Tourist eVisa (Single Entry)
- **Single Entry (30 days):** For tourism, cultural tours, and sightseeing. Government fee: $112 USD.

## Ethiopia eVisa Requirements

To apply for an Ethiopia eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Ethiopia
- Return flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

## Travel Tips for Ethiopia

- **Best Time to Visit:** October to May (dry season) is ideal for sightseeing. June-September is the rainy season.
- **Popular Destinations:** Addis Ababa, Lalibela, Simien Mountains, Axum, Lake Tana, Omo Valley.
- **Local Language:** Amharic (English widely spoken in tourist areas)
- **Currency:** Ethiopian Birr (ETB)
- **Safety:** Ethiopia is generally safe for tourists, especially in popular areas. Stay informed about travel advisories and take standard precautions.
- **Cultural Sites:** Visit the rock-hewn churches, ancient ruins, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
- **Trekking:** The Simien Mountains offer world-class trekking opportunities.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the Immigration Department in Addis Ababa or other major cities.
