# Gabon eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Gabon?

Gabon is a country of pristine rainforests, diverse wildlife, and stunning beaches. From Loango National Park to the capital Libreville, Gabon offers unique adventures for eco-tourists and nature lovers. Whether you're interested in wildlife viewing, beach activities, or cultural exploration, Gabon is a hidden gem in Central Africa.

## Gabon eVisa Overview

The Gabon eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Gabon easier and more efficient.

## Gabon eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Gabon eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Gabon
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English or French. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

- **Best Time to Visit:** May to September is the dry season, ideal for wildlife viewing and outdoor activities.
- **Popular Destinations:** Libreville, Loango National Park, Ivindo National Park, Port-Gentil.
