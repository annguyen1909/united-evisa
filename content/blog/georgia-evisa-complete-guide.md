# Georgia eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Georgia?

Georgia is a country of breathtaking mountains, ancient cities, and vibrant culture. From Tbilisi's old town to the Caucasus peaks, Georgia offers a unique blend of European and Asian influences. Whether you're interested in wine tourism, mountain adventures, or cultural exploration, Georgia is a hidden gem for travelers.

## Georgia eVisa Overview

The Georgia eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Georgia easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Georgia eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Georgia. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism or business purposes. Some countries may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Georgia eVisas

### Business eVisa
- **Multiple Entries (30 days):** For business meetings, trade opportunities, and investment exploration. Government fee: $50 USD.

## Georgia eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Georgia eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Georgia
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

## Travel Tips for Georgia

- **Best Time to Visit:** Spring (April–June) and autumn (September–November) offer pleasant weather. Summer is great for mountains; winter for skiing.
- **Popular Destinations:** Tbilisi, Batumi, Svaneti, Kakheti wine region, Mtskheta.
- **Local Language:** Georgian
- **Currency:** Georgian Lari (GEL)
- **Safety:** Georgia is generally safe for tourists with low crime rates and friendly locals.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the Public Service Hall for more information.
