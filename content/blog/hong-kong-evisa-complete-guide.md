# Hong Kong eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Hong Kong?

Hong Kong is a vibrant international city known for its stunning skyline, diverse culture, and world-class shopping. From Victoria Peak to bustling street markets, Hong Kong offers unforgettable experiences. Whether you're interested in business, tourism, or cultural exploration, Hong Kong is a gateway to Asia.

## Hong Kong eVisa Overview

The Hong Kong eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Hong Kong easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Hong Kong eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Hong Kong. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, or transit purposes. Some nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Hong Kong eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Hong Kong eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Hong Kong
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.
