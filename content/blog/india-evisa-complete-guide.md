# India eVisa Complete Guide: Everything You Need to Know

India's eVisa system has revolutionized travel to this incredible destination, making it easier than ever to obtain authorization for your visit. Whether you're planning a spiritual journey, business trip, or vacation, this comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know about the India eVisa.

## What is India eVisa?

The India eVisa (electronic visa) is an official travel authorization that allows eligible foreign nationals to enter India for tourism, business, medical treatment, or conference purposes. Launched in 2014, this digital visa system has streamlined the application process, eliminating the need to visit Indian consulates or embassies.

## Types of India eVisa

### Tourist eVisa
Perfect for leisure travelers, sightseeing, and visiting friends or relatives.
- **Duration**: 30 days to 5 years
- **Entries**: Single, double, or multiple
- **Most Popular**: 30-day double entry

### Business eVisa
Designed for business meetings, conferences, and commercial activities.
- **Duration**: Up to 1 year
- **Entries**: Multiple
- **Activities**: Meetings, conferences, trade fairs

### Medical eVisa
For medical treatment and accompanying attendants.
- **Duration**: Up to 60 days
- **Entries**: Triple
- **Accompanists**: Up to 2 medical attendants

## Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for an India eVisa, you must:

1. **Hold a qualifying passport** from one of 170+ eligible countries
2. **Have a valid passport** with at least 6 months validity
3. **Travel for permitted purposes** (tourism, business, medical, or conference)
4. **Meet entry requirements** at designated airports and seaports

## Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare Required Documents

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I extend my India eVisa?
No, India eVisas cannot be extended. You must exit the country and apply for a new visa if needed.

### What if my application is rejected?
Rejections are rare with proper documentation. Common reasons include incomplete forms, poor document quality, or eligibility issues.

### Can I use eVisa for multiple trips?
Depends on your visa type. Multiple-entry visas allow several trips within the validity period.

### Is eVisa valid for all entry points?
No, eVisa is only valid for designated airports and seaports. Land borders require regular visas.
