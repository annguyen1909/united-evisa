## India eVisa Overview

The India eVisa is an electronic travel authorization that allows eligible foreign nationals to enter India for tourism, business, medical treatment, or conferences. Introduced to simplify the visa process, the eVisa can be obtained entirely online, eliminating the need to visit embassies or consulates.

## Who Needs an India eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter India. The eVisa system is available to travelers from over 170 countries. Some neighboring countries may have different requirements, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## India eVisa Requirements

To apply for an India eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in India
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- For business visas: Invitation letter from the Indian company
- For medical visas: Medical letter from a recognized hospital

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English.
