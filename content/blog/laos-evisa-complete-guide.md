# Laos eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Laos?

Laos is a landlocked gem in Southeast Asia, known for its serene Buddhist temples, French colonial architecture, and pristine natural beauty. From the UNESCO World Heritage city of Luang Prabang to the mysterious Plain of Jars, Laos offers authentic cultural experiences and untouched landscapes. Whether you're seeking spiritual enlightenment, adventure, or cultural immersion, Laos provides a peaceful escape from the modern world.

## Laos eVisa Overview

The Laos eVisa system, launched in July 2019, allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Laos easier and more efficient for tourists worldwide.

## Who Needs a Laos eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Laos. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism purposes. Citizens of some ASEAN countries may be exempt from visa requirements for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Laos eVisas

### Tourist eVisa
- **Single Entry (30 days):** For tourism, temple exploration, and cultural experiences. Government fee: $81 USD.

*Note: Laos primarily offers tourist eVisas through the online system. Business and other visa types may require embassy applications.*

## Laos eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Laos eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Laos
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- Travel insurance (recommended)

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your nationality.
