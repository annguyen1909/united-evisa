# Moldova eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Moldova?

Moldova is Europe's hidden gem, known for its charming villages, world-class vineyards, and rich history. From the capital city Chisinau to the ancient Orheiul Vechi Monastery, Moldova offers authentic European experiences away from the crowds. Whether you're interested in wine tourism, cultural exploration, or discovering medieval sites, Moldova provides unique adventures for discerning travelers.

## Moldova eVisa Overview

The Moldova eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Moldova easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Moldova eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Moldova. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism and business purposes. EU citizens and some other nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Moldova eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Moldova eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Moldova
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

## Travel Tips for Moldova

- **Best Time to Visit:** May to September offers warm weather perfect for wine tours and outdoor activities. October is harvest season - ideal for wine enthusiasts.
- **Popular Destinations:** Chisinau, Orheiul Vechi, Cricova Winery, Milestii Mici, Soroca Fortress.
- **Local Language:** Moldovan (Romanian), Russian (English in tourist areas)
- **Currency:** Moldovan Leu (MDL)
- **Safety:** Moldova is generally safe for tourists with low crime rates and friendly locals.
- **Wine Tourism:** Moldova is famous for its wine cellars and vineyards. Don't miss underground wine cities like Cricova and Milestii Mici.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the local immigration office for more information.
