# Pakistan eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Pakistan?

Pakistan is a country of incredible diversity, from the towering peaks of the Karakoram and Himalayas to the ancient Indus Valley civilization sites. From the bustling city of Karachi to the scenic valleys of Hunza, Pakistan offers breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Whether you're interested in mountaineering, cultural exploration, or business opportunities, Pakistan provides unique experiences for adventurous travelers.

## Pakistan eVisa Overview

The Pakistan eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Pakistan easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Pakistan eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Pakistan. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism and business purposes. Some nationalities may have special arrangements, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Pakistan eVisas

### Tourist eVisa
- **Single Entry (3 months):** For cultural heritage tours and mountain adventures. Government fee: $90 USD.

### Business eVisa
- **Single Entry (3 months):** For business meetings and trade exhibitions. Government fee: $130 USD.
- **Multiple Entries (1 year):** For extended business travel and partnerships. Government fee: $180 USD.

*Note: Stay duration is typically 30 days per entry, but visa validity allows applications within the specified period.*

## Pakistan eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Pakistan eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Pakistan
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose and nationality.

## Travel Tips for Pakistan

- **Best Time to Visit:** October to April offers pleasant weather. Summer can be very hot in plains; ideal for northern mountain regions.
- **Popular Destinations:** Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi, Hunza Valley, Skardu, Gilgit, Swat Valley.
- **Local Language:** Urdu and English (English widely spoken in cities)
- **Currency:** Pakistani Rupee (PKR)
- **Safety:** Security situation varies by region. Check current travel advisories and use reputable tour operators for remote areas.
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** Pakistan is an Islamic country. Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites and rural areas.
- **Mountain Adventures:** Pakistan offers world-class trekking and mountaineering opportunities in the Karakoram and Himalayas.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the passport office for more information.
