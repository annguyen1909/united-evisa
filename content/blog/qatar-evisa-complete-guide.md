# Qatar eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

Qatar is a modern Gulf state that seamlessly blends traditional Arabian culture with cutting-edge architecture and luxury experiences. From the futuristic skyline of Doha to the stunning Museum of Islamic Art, Qatar offers world-class attractions and hospitality. Whether you're interested in business, cultural exploration, or luxury shopping, Qatar provides unforgettable Middle Eastern experiences.

## Qatar eVisa Overview

The Qatar eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Qatar easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Qatar eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Qatar. Citizens of GCC countries can enter without a visa. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business meetings, family visits, and cultural exploration. Always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Qatar eVisas

### Tourist eVisa
- **Single Entry (30 days):** For tourism, family visits, and cultural exploration. Government fee: $90 USD.

*Note: Qatar primarily offers tourist eVisas through the online system. Business and other visa types may require embassy applications.*

## Qatar eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Qatar eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Qatar
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- Travel insurance (recommended)

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English or Arabic. Additional documents may be required depending on your nationality.
