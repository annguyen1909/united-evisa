## Saudi Arabia eVisa Overview

The Saudi Arabia eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Saudi Arabia easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Saudi Arabia eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Saudi Arabia. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, family visits, and cultural exploration. Religious pilgrimage (Hajj and Umrah) requires separate visa types. Always check your eligibility before applying.

## Saudi Arabia eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Saudi Arabia eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Saudi Arabia
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- Travel insurance (mandatory)

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English or Arabic. Additional documents may be required depending on your nationality.
