# South Africa eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit South Africa?

South Africa is a captivating destination known for its incredible wildlife, diverse landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. From the iconic Table Mountain in Cape Town to the Big Five safaris in Kruger National Park, South Africa offers world-class experiences. Whether you're interested in safari adventures, wine tourism, business opportunities, or cultural exploration, South Africa provides unforgettable African experiences.

## South Africa eVisa Overview

The South Africa eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to South Africa easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a South Africa eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter South Africa. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism and business purposes. Some nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## South Africa eVisa Requirements

To apply for a South Africa eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in South Africa
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.
