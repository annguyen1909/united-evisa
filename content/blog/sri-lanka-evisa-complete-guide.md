# Sri Lanka eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka, the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a tropical paradise known for its ancient temples, lush tea plantations, and pristine beaches. From the cultural triangle of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa to the hill country of Kandy and the coastal beauty of Galle, Sri Lanka offers incredible diversity. Whether you're interested in cultural exploration, wildlife safaris, beach holidays, or tea plantation tours, Sri Lanka provides unforgettable experiences.

## Sri Lanka eVisa Overview

The Sri Lanka eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Sri Lanka easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Sri Lanka eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Sri Lanka. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, and transit purposes. Some nationalities may be visa-exempt for short stays, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Types of Sri Lanka eVisas

### Tourist eVisa
- **Double Entries:** For temple exploration and beach holidays. Government fee: $85 USD.

### Business eVisa
- **Multiple Entries (30 days):** For business meetings and trade exhibitions. Government fee: $80 USD.

### Transit eVisa
- **Single Entry (48 hours):** For airport transit and quick connections. Government fee: $30 USD

*Note: All eVisas have a validity period of 180 days from the date of issue.*

## Sri Lanka eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Sri Lanka eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Sri Lanka
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

## Travel Tips for Sri Lanka

- **Best Time to Visit:** December to March is ideal for the west and south coasts. May to September is better for the east coast and cultural triangle.
- **Popular Destinations:** Colombo, Kandy, Galle, Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, Nuwara Eliya, Yala National Park.
- **Local Language:** Sinhala and Tamil (English widely spoken)
- **Currency:** Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)
- **Safety:** Sri Lanka is generally safe for tourists. Exercise standard precautions and be respectful of local customs.
- **Cultural Sites:** Sri Lanka has eight UNESCO World Heritage Sites including ancient cities and sacred temples.
- **Tea Tourism:** Visit the hill country for world-famous Ceylon tea plantation tours.
- **Wildlife:** Sri Lanka offers excellent opportunities to see elephants, leopards, and diverse bird species.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the Department of Immigration and Emigration.
