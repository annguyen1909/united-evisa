## What is Taiwan eVisa?

The Taiwan eVisa is an electronic travel authorization that allows eligible foreign nationals to enter Taiwan for tourism, business, or transit purposes. Implemented to simplify the visa application process, the Taiwan eVisa eliminates the need to visit a consulate or embassy in person.

### Key Benefits of Taiwan eVisa:
- **100% Online Application** - Apply from anywhere in the world
- **Fast Processing** - Get your visa in 3-5 business days
- **Multiple Entry Options** - Single or multiple entry visas available
- **Digital Convenience** - No physical passport submission required
- **24/7 Application** - Apply any time, day or night

## Taiwan eVisa Types and Requirements

### 1. Tourist eVisa
**Purpose:** Tourism, sightseeing, visiting friends and family
**Validity:** 30 days single entry or 90 days multiple entry
**Stay Duration:** Up to 30 days per entry

**Requirements:**
- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Completed online application form
- Recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Taiwan
- Round-trip flight itinerary
- Financial proof (bank statements)
- Travel insurance (recommended)

### 2. Business eVisa
**Purpose:** Business meetings, conferences, commercial activities
**Validity:** 90 days multiple entry
**Stay Duration:** Up to 30 days per entry

**Additional Requirements:**
- Business invitation letter from Taiwan company
- Company registration documents
- Purpose of visit explanation
- Business license of applicant's company

### 3. Transit eVisa
**Purpose:** Transit through Taiwan to third country
**Validity:** 7 days single entry
**Stay Duration:** Up to 7 days

**Requirements:**
- Confirmed onward ticket to third destination
- Valid visa for final destination (if required)
- Proof of accommodation during transit

## Step-by-Step Taiwan eVisa Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare Required Documents
Gather all necessary documents according to your visa type:
- **Passport:** Must be valid for at least 6 months
- **Photo:** Recent passport-size photo (white background)
- **Flight Itinerary:** Round-trip booking confirmation
- **Accommodation Proof:** Hotel booking or invitation letter
- **Financial Proof:** Bank statements for last 3 months
- **Travel Insurance:** Coverage of at least $100,000 USD

### Step 2: Complete Online Application
1. Visit the official Taiwan eVisa portal
2. Select your visa type (Tourist/Business/Transit)
3. Fill out the application form completely
4. Upload required documents in specified formats
5. Review all information carefully

### Step 3: Pay Visa Fee
- **Tourist eVisa:** $37 USD
- **Business eVisa:** $63 USD  
- **Transit eVisa:** $26 USD
- Payment methods: Credit card, debit card

### Step 4: Submit Application
- Double-check all information
- Submit your application online
- Save your application reference number
- You'll receive an email confirmation

### Step 5: Track Application Status
- Use your reference number to track progress
- Check your email regularly for updates
- Processing typically takes 3-5 business days

### Step 6: Receive Your eVisa
- Download your approved eVisa
- Print a copy for travel
- Check all details are correct
- Keep both digital and physical copies

### Renewal Requirements:
- Taiwan eVisa cannot be renewed
- Must apply for new visa if needed
- Exit and re-enter Taiwan for multiple visits

### Extension Options:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I apply for Taiwan eVisa if I'm already in Taiwan?
A: No, you must apply for Taiwan eVisa before traveling to Taiwan. Applications cannot be submitted from within Taiwan.

### Q: What if my Taiwan eVisa application is rejected?
A: You can reapply after addressing the rejection reasons. Common causes include incomplete documentation or insufficient financial proof.

### Q: Can I work in Taiwan with a tourist eVisa?
A: No, tourist eVisas do not permit employment. You need a work permit and appropriate visa for employment in Taiwan.

### Q: Is travel insurance mandatory for Taiwan eVisa?
A: While not always mandatory, travel insurance is highly recommended and may be required for certain nationalities.
