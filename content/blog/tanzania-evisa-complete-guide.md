# Tanzania eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Tanzania?

Tanzania is home to some of Africa's most iconic wildlife destinations and natural wonders. From the Serengeti's Great Migration to the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro and the pristine beaches of Zanzibar, Tanzania offers world-class safari experiences and diverse landscapes. Whether you're interested in wildlife photography, mountain climbing, beach holidays, or cultural exploration, Tanzania provides unforgettable African adventures.

## Tanzania eVisa Overview

The Tanzania eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Tanzania easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Tanzania eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Tanzania. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, and transit purposes. Some East African countries may have visa exemptions, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Tanzania eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Tanzania eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Tanzania
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents
- **For transit eVisa:** Proof of onward travel

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

## Travel Tips for Tanzania

- **Best Time to Visit:** June to October (dry season) is ideal for wildlife viewing. December to March is good for calving season in Serengeti.
- **Popular Destinations:** Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Mount Kilimanjaro, Zanzibar, Tarangire National Park.
- **Local Language:** Swahili and English
- **Currency:** Tanzanian Shilling (TZS)
- **Safety:** Tanzania is generally safe for tourists. Use reputable tour operators for safaris and follow local guidance.
- **Health:** Yellow fever vaccination may be required. Malaria prophylaxis is recommended.
- **Safari Experiences:** Tanzania offers some of the world's best wildlife viewing opportunities.
- **Zanzibar:** Beautiful beaches and historic Stone Town make Zanzibar perfect for post-safari relaxation.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the immigration office for more information.
