## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long is Turkey eVisa valid for US citizens?
A: Turkey eVisa is valid for 180 days from the date of issue, allowing stays up to 90 days within that period.

### Q: Can I work in Turkey with a tourist eVisa?
A: No, tourist eVisa does not permit employment. You need a work permit and appropriate visa for employment.

### Q: What if my Turkey eVisa application is rejected?
A: You can reapply after addressing rejection reasons or apply for a regular visa at a Turkish consulate. Our support team can help identify and resolve issues.

### Q: Can I enter Turkey multiple times with single entry eVisa?
A: No, single entry eVisa allows only one entry. Choose multiple entry if you plan to exit and re-enter Turkey during your 180-day validity period.

### Q: Is travel insurance required for Turkey eVisa?
A: Travel insurance is not mandatory but highly recommended for medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and other unforeseen circumstances.

### Q: Can I extend my stay in Turkey?
A: No, tourist eVisas cannot be extended. You must exit Turkey before your visa expires and can reapply for a new visa if needed.

### Q: What happens if I overstay my visa?
A: Overstaying can result in fines, entry bans, and other serious consequences. Always exit before your visa expires.

### Q: Do I need vaccinations for Turkey?
A: No special vaccinations are required for Turkey, but routine vaccinations are recommended. Consult your doctor before traveling.
