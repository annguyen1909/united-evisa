# Turkey eVisa: Quick Guide for US Citizens

Turkey's stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture make it a top destination for American travelers. With the Turkey eVisa system, US citizens can easily obtain travel authorization online, making the journey to this fascinating country more accessible than ever.

## Turkey eVisa for US Citizens

The Turkey eVisa is an electronic travel authorization that allows US citizens to enter Turkey for tourism or business purposes. This digital visa system eliminates the need for embassy visits and provides a convenient online application process.

### Key Benefits
- **100% online process**
- **Fast approval** (usually within 24 hours)
- **Multiple entry** allowed
- **90-day validity** with 30-day stays

## Eligibility Requirements

US citizens can apply for Turkey eVisa if they:
- Hold a valid US passport
- Plan to visit for tourism or business
- Have a passport valid for at least 150 days
- Possess a return ticket

## Application Process

### Step 1: Online Application
1. Visit the official Turkey eVisa website
2. Select "United States" as your country
3. Fill out the application form
4. Upload required documents

### Step 2: Payment
- **Visa fee**: $50 USD
- **Service fee**: Additional charges may apply
- Payment via credit/debit card

### Step 3: Receive eVisa
- Processing time: 24-72 hours
- eVisa sent via email
- Print and carry during travel

## Required Documents

### Essential Documents
- **Valid US passport** (150+ days validity)
- **Email address** for correspondence
- **Credit card** for payment

### Photograph Requirements
- Recent passport-style photo
- Color photograph
- White background
- JPEG format

## Important Guidelines

### Entry Requirements
- Must arrive within 180 days of approval
- Valid for 180 days from issue date
- Allows multiple entries
- Each stay: maximum 30 days

### Prohibited Activities
- Employment or work
- Permanent residence
- Journalism (requires special permit)

## Common Questions

### Can I work with a Turkey eVisa?
No, the eVisa is strictly for tourism and business activities, not employment.

### Is the eVisa valid for all entry points?
Yes, the eVisa is accepted at all Turkish airports and land borders.

### What if I stay longer than 30 days?
Overstaying can result in fines and future entry restrictions.

- Purchase travel insurance
- Research local customs

- Carry proof of accommodation
