## UK e-Visa vs. Visa on Arrival: Which One Should You Choose?

The UK offers both e-Visas and limited Visa on Arrival options. Which is right for you?

## Key Differences

| Feature         | e-Visa           | Visa on Arrival      |
| --------------- | ---------------- | -------------------- |
| Application     | Online           | At border (limited)  |
| Entry Points    | Most major ports | Select airports only |
| Processing Time | 3-5 days         | Immediate            |
| Fee             | Online payment   | Pay at border        |
| Duration        | 30-180 days      | Varies               |

---

## Pros and Cons

### ✅ e-Visa

- Apply from home
- No waiting at the border

### ✅ Visa on Arrival

- For urgent travel
- Limited eligibility

## Final Verdict

If eligible, the e-Visa is easier and faster. Use Visa on Arrival only if necessary.
