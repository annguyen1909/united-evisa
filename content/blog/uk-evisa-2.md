## UK e-Visa Entry Points: Airports, Land Borders & Seaports

Before applying for your UK e-Visa, know where you can enter the country.

## Approved Entry Points

UK e-Visas are accepted at all major international airports, land borders, and seaports, including:

- **Heathrow Airport**
- **Gatwick Airport**
- **Manchester Airport**
- **London St Pancras (Eurostar)**
- **Dover Seaport**

## Important Travel Tip

You must specify your entry point in your application. Changing it later may not be possible.

## Can I Enter the UK From Anywhere?

No, only enter through listed checkpoints. Unauthorized entry may result in denial.
