## UK e-Visa Requirements: What You Need to Prepare

Planning to visit the UK? The e-Visa system streamlines the process, but you’ll need to meet some basic requirements for approval.

## Basic Eligibility for a UK e-Visa

The UK offers e-Visas to citizens of select countries. Check the [official list](https://www.gov.uk/) to confirm your eligibility.

## Required Documents for a UK e-Visa

To apply for a UK e-Visa, prepare the following:

- **Valid passport** (at least 6 months validity)
- **Digital passport photo** (white background, no glasses)
- **Scanned passport data page**
- **Valid email address**
- **Credit or debit card** for payment

## Tips to Avoid Rejection

- Double-check all personal details
- Match the passport photo requirements
- Upload clear, valid files
- Apply well before your travel date

## Who Cannot Apply for an e-Visa?

- Citizens from countries not listed under the UK’s e-Visa program
- Travelers with diplomatic or official passports
- Individuals with past visa violations or bans in the UK

## Final Note

Meeting all requirements increases your chance of fast approval. Always verify the latest criteria from the official site before applying.
