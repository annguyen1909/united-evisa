## United Kingdom eVisa Overview

The United Kingdom eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to the UK easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a United Kingdom eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter the United Kingdom. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, study, and medical treatment purposes. ## United Kingdom eVisa Requirements

To apply for a United Kingdom eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in the UK
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents
- **For study eVisa:** Letter of acceptance from educational institution
- **For medical eVisa:** Medical appointment confirmation

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.
