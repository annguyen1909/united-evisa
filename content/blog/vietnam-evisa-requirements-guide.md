# Vietnam eVisa Requirements and Application Guide

Vietnam's eVisa system has streamlined the visa application process for travelers worldwide. Whether you're planning to explore Ho Chi Minh City's bustling streets, cruise through Ha Long Bay, or trek in Sapa, this guide will help you navigate the Vietnam eVisa application successfully.

## What is Vietnam eVisa?

The Vietnam eVisa is an electronic visa that allows foreign nationals to enter Vietnam for tourism, business, transit, or other purposes. This digital system replaced the traditional visa-on-arrival process for many countries, offering a more convenient and secure application method.

## Types of Vietnam eVisa

### Single Entry eVisa
- **Duration**: 30 days
- **Entries**: One entry only
- **Purpose**: Tourism, business, transit

### Multiple Entry eVisa
- **Duration**: 90 days
- **Entries**: Multiple entries allowed
- **Purpose**: Business, long-term tourism

## Eligibility and Requirements

### Eligible Countries
Vietnam eVisa is available to citizens of 80+ countries including:
- United States
- United Kingdom
- Australia
- Canada
- European Union countries
- And many more

### Basic Requirements
1. **Valid passport** (6+ months validity)
2. **Passport photo** (recent, color)
3. **Travel purpose** documentation
4. **Financial proof** (bank statements)

## Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare Documents
Gather all required documents before starting your application:
- Scanned passport bio page
- Digital passport photograph
- Travel itinerary
- Proof of accommodation

### Step 2: Complete Online Form
1. Access the official Vietnam eVisa portal
2. Select your nationality
3. Choose visa type and duration
4. Fill out personal information
5. Upload required documents

### Step 3: Pay Visa Fee
- **Single entry**: $25 USD
- **Multiple entry**: $50 USD
- Payment via credit/debit card

### Step 4: Wait for Processing
- **Standard processing**: 3 business days
- **Rush processing**: 1-2 business days
- Receive eVisa via email

## Document Requirements

### Passport Requirements
- Valid for at least 6 months
- At least 2 blank pages
- Clear, high-quality scan
- All information visible

### Photograph Specifications
- **Size**: 4cm x 6cm
- **Background**: White
- **Format**: JPEG
- **Quality**: High resolution
- **Recent**: Taken within 6 months

### Supporting Documents
- **Accommodation proof**: Hotel booking or invitation letter
- **Travel itinerary**: Flight bookings
- **Financial proof**: Bank statements (last 3 months)
- **Travel insurance**: Recommended but not mandatory

## Processing Times and Fees

### Standard Processing
- **Timeline**: 3 business days
- **Cost**: Government fee + service fee
- **Delivery**: Email notification

### Rush Processing
- **Timeline**: 1-2 business days
- **Additional cost**: Rush fee applies
- **Availability**: Subject to government processing

### Government Fees
- **30-day single entry**: $25 USD
- **90-day multiple entry**: $50 USD
- **Service fees**: Additional charges apply

## Important Guidelines

### Validity Period
- eVisa is valid from the date of approval
- Must enter Vietnam within 30 days of issue
- Cannot be extended or converted

### Stay Duration
- **Single entry**: Maximum 30 days
- **Multiple entry**: Maximum 90 days total
- **Overstay**: Penalties and fines apply

### Prohibited Activities
- Employment without work permit
- Journalism without accreditation
- Activities against national security
