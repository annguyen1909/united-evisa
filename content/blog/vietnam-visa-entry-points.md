## Vietnam e-Visa Entry Points: Airports, Land Borders & Seaports

Before applying for your Vietnam e-Visa, it’s essential to know where you can enter the country using it.

## Approved Entry Points for Vietnam e-Visa Holders

Vietnam e-Visas are accepted at **33 international checkpoints**, including airports, land borders, and seaports.

### Major Airports

- **Hanoi (Noi Bai International Airport)**  
- **Ho Chi Minh City (Tan Son Nhat International Airport)**  
- **Da Nang International Airport**  
- **Cam Ranh International Airport (Nha Trang)**  
- **Phu Quoc International Airport**

### Land Border Gates

- **Moc Bai (Cambodia – Tay Ninh Province)**  
- **Lao Bao (Laos – Quang Tri Province)**  
- **Na Meo, Cha Lo, and more**

### Seaports

- **Da Nang Seaport**  
- **Hai Phong Seaport**  
- **Hon Gai (Ha Long Bay)**  
- **Nha Trang Seaport**

## Important Travel Tip

When applying, you **must choose your entry and exit points**. These **cannot be changed** after submission, so plan carefully.

## Can I Enter Vietnam From Anywhere?

No, only enter through listed checkpoints. Entering from an unauthorized location may result in **denial of entry** even with a valid e-Visa.
