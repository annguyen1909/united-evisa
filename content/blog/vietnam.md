## How Fast Can I Get a Vietnam e-Visa?

Vietnam's e-Visa system is designed to be efficient and traveler-friendly. If you're planning a trip, knowing how quickly you can obtain your e-Visa will help you prepare and avoid unnecessary stress.

## What Is the Typical Processing Time for a Vietnam e-Visa?

The official processing time for a Vietnam e-Visa is typically **3 working days** (excluding weekends and Vietnamese public holidays). However, during peak travel seasons or due to high application volumes, it may take slightly longer.

You will receive an email notification once your visa is approved, and you can also check the status on the official immigration portal using your registration code.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I apply for a Vietnam e-Visa on the weekend?

You can **submit your application any day**, but it will only be processed on working days (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays).

### What is the maximum stay on a Vietnam e-Visa?

The standard e-Visa allows a **maximum stay of 30 days** for a **single entry**.

### Can I extend my e-Visa once I'm in Vietnam?

Currently, extensions are **not guaranteed**. If you wish to stay longer, you must exit and re-apply for a new visa.

### Is the approval time the same for all nationalities?

While most nationalities experience the same 3-day timeframe, **some countries** may face additional checks. Always check the latest eligibility and processing info on the official site.
