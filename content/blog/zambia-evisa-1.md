## Zambia e-Visa Requirements: What You Need to Prepare

Traveling to Zambia? The e-Visa system makes it simple, but you must meet certain requirements.

## Basic Eligibility

Zambia offers e-Visas to citizens of many countries. Check the [official list](https://eservices.zambiaimmigration.gov.zm/) for eligibility.

## Required Documents

- **Valid passport** (at least 6 months validity)
- **Digital passport photo**
- **Scanned passport data page**
- **Valid email address**
- **Credit or debit card** for payment

## Tips to Avoid Rejection

- Double-check all details
- Follow photo requirements
- Upload clear, valid files
- Apply early

## Who Cannot Apply?

- Citizens not listed for e-Visa
- Travelers with diplomatic passports
- Those with past visa violations

## Final Note

Check the latest requirements before applying.
