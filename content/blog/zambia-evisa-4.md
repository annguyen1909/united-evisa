## Zambia e-Visa vs. Visa on Arrival: Which One Should You Choose?

Zambia offers both e-Visas and Visa on Arrival. Which is best for you?

## Key Differences

| Feature         | e-Visa           | Visa on Arrival         |
| --------------- | ---------------- | ----------------------- |
| Application     | Online           | At border               |
| Entry Points    | Most major ports | Select airports/borders |
| Processing Time | 3-5 days         | Immediate               |
| Fee             | Online payment   | Pay at border           |
| Duration        | 30-90 days       | Varies                  |

## Pros and Cons

### ✅ e-Visa

- Apply before travel
- No waiting at the border

### ✅ Visa on Arrival

- For urgent travel
- Available to many nationalities

## Final Verdict

If eligible, the e-Visa is more convenient. Use Visa on Arrival if you need to travel
