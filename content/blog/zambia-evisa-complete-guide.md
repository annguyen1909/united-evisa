# Zambia eVisa Complete Guide: Requirements, Application, and Travel Tips

## Why Visit Zambia?

Zambia is famous for Victoria Falls, one of the world's most spectacular waterfalls, and excellent wildlife safaris. From the thundering waters of "The Smoke that Thunders" to the diverse wildlife of South Luangwa National Park and the adventure activities in Livingstone, Zambia offers authentic African experiences. Whether you're interested in wildlife viewing, adventure activities, cultural exploration, or natural wonders, Zambia provides unforgettable safari adventures.

## Zambia eVisa Overview

The Zambia eVisa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply for and obtain an electronic visa online before traveling. This convenient process eliminates the need to visit an embassy or consulate, making travel to Zambia easier and more efficient.

## Who Needs a Zambia eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Zambia. The eVisa is available to travelers from eligible countries for tourism, business, and transit purposes. Some Southern African countries may have visa exemptions, so always check your eligibility before applying.

## Zambia eVisa Requirements

To apply for a Zambia eVisa, you will need:
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay
- A recent passport-size photo
- Proof of accommodation in Zambia
- Return or onward flight tickets
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- **For business eVisa:** Invitation letter or supporting documents
- **For transit eVisa:** Proof of onward travel

All documents should be clear, legible, and in English. Additional documents may be required depending on your travel purpose.

Zambia eVisa holders can enter through Kenneth Kaunda International Airport (Lusaka), Harry Mwanga Nkumbula International Airport (Livingstone), and designated land borders with neighboring countries. Always carry your eVisa approval letter and passport when traveling.

## Travel Tips for Zambia

- **Best Time to Visit:** May to October offers dry weather ideal for wildlife viewing and Victoria Falls visits.
- **Popular Destinations:** Victoria Falls, Livingstone, Lusaka, South Luangwa National Park, Lower Zambezi National Park.
- **Local Language:** English and local languages
- **Currency:** Zambian Kwacha (ZMW)
- **Safety:** Zambia is generally safe for tourists. Use reputable tour operators and follow local guidance.
- **Wildlife:** Excellent opportunities to see elephants, lions, leopards, and diverse bird species.
- **Victoria Falls:** One of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, best viewed during dry season.
- **Adventure Activities:** Bungee jumping, white-water rafting, helicopter flights, and sunset cruises.
- **Health:** Yellow fever vaccination may be required. Malaria prophylaxis is recommended.
- **Visa Extensions:** Extensions are possible for valid reasons. Visit the immigration office for more information.
