## Standard Armenia eVisa Processing Times

### Government Processing Framework

The Armenian government's official processing time for eVisa applications is **5 working days** from the date of submission. This timeframe applies to all visa types, including:

- Tourist eVisa (21 days or 120 days)
- Business eVisa (21 days or 120 days)
- All eligible nationalities

### What "Working Days" Means

It's important to understand that processing time is calculated in **working days only**, which means:

- Weekends (Saturday and Sunday) are not counted
- Armenian public holidays are excluded
- International holidays may also affect processing
- Processing starts the day after submission
