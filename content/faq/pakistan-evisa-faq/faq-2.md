## Essential Documents for Pakistan eVisa

### 1. Valid Passport

**Passport Requirements:**
- Must be valid for at least 6 months from intended entry date
- Must have at least 2 blank pages for entry/exit stamps
- Should be in good condition without damage
- Biographical page must be clearly readable

**Passport Scan Requirements:**
- High-resolution color scan of biographical page
- All text and numbers must be clearly visible
- No shadows, glares, or reflections
- File format: JPEG or PDF
- Maximum file size: 2MB

### 2. Passport-Size Photograph

**Photo Specifications:**
- Recent photograph (taken within last 6 months)
- White or light-colored background
- Face should occupy 70-80% of the photo
- Direct frontal view with neutral expression
- No glasses, hats, or head coverings (religious exceptions apply)

**Technical Requirements:**
- Size: 35mm x 45mm (passport size)
- Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI
- Color photograph (not black and white)
- Clear, sharp image quality
- File format: JPEG
- Maximum file size: 1MB

### 3. Proof of Accommodation

**Accepted Accommodation Proof:**
- Hotel booking confirmations
- Guest house or hostel reservations
- Invitation letter from host (if staying with friends/family)
- Rental property agreements
- Tour package accommodation details

**Requirements:**
- Must show applicant's name
- Include full address and contact details
- Cover entire duration of stay
- Confirmed booking (not pending or tentative)
- English translation if in local language

### 4. Return Flight Itinerary

**Flight Documentation:**
- Round-trip flight reservation or tickets
- Clear entry and exit dates from Pakistan
- Passenger name matching passport exactly
- Airline confirmation number
- Valid ticket or confirmed booking

**Important Notes:**
- One-way tickets may require additional documentation
- Overland travel requires border crossing documentation
- Transit passengers need onward travel proof
- Flexible tickets accepted with confirmed dates

### 5. Financial Statements

**Bank Statements:**
- Last 3 months of bank statements
- Minimum balance: $1,000-2,000 USD equivalent
- Account holder name matching passport
- Official bank letterhead and stamps
- English translation if in local language

**Alternative Financial Proof:**
- Credit card statements
- Sponsorship letters with financial guarantee
- Traveler's checks or cash declarations
- Employment letter with salary details
- Business financial statements

## Document Quality Standards

### Photo Quality Requirements

**Professional Standards:**
- Studio-quality lighting and background
- Sharp focus on facial features
- Natural skin tones and colors
- No digital enhancement or filters
- Professional composition and framing

**Common Photo Mistakes to Avoid:**
- Selfies or informal photos
- Group photos or multiple people
- Blurry or out-of-focus images
- Incorrect background colors
- Improper lighting or shadows

### Document Scanning Best Practices

**High-Quality Scans:**
- Use scanner or high-resolution camera
- Ensure documents are flat and straight
- Adequate lighting without shadows
- Clean, undamaged original documents
- Color scans for better clarity

**File Management:**

- Check file sizes meet requirements
- Verify all text is readable
- Maintain original document ratios
- Keep backup copies
