## Who Needs a Vietnam Visa?

### Foreign Nationals Requiring a Visa

Most international travelers need a visa to enter Vietnam, including citizens from:

**Major Regions Requiring Visas:**
- **North America**: United States, Canada, Mexico
- **Europe**: All EU countries, UK, Norway, Switzerland, Russia
- **Asia-Pacific**: China, Japan, India, Australia, New Zealand, South Korea
- **Middle East**: Most countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar
- **Africa**: All African countries requiring prior visa arrangement
- **South America**: Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Colombia

### Visa-Free Entry

Limited visa-free access is available for:
- ASEAN citizens (up to 30 days)
- Some European countries (15-30 days for specific purposes)
- South Korea, Japan (15 days)
- Russia, Belarus (15 days)
